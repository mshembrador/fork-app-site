Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Overview

Fork is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

Fork does not collect any personal data. All screen time monitoring and activity data remains exclusively on your device. We do not have servers, user accounts, or cloud storage.

Screen Time Access

Fork requests access to Screen Time data solely to:

  • Monitor your app usage based on limits you set
  • Display app names and icons in your activity journal
  • Track time intervals between app usage

This data never leaves your device and cannot be accessed by us or any third party.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. Data is included in your personal iCloud backups if you have iCloud Backup enabled, but we do not have access to these backups.

Third-Party Services

Fork does not use any:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Tracking tools
  • Social media integrations
  • External servers or APIs

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any information with third parties because we do not collect any information in the first place.

Children's Privacy

Fork is designed for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will notify you through an app update description.

Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, contact: support@fork-app.app
